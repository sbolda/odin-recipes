Pizza margherita

What is pizza?

Pizza Margherita is a traditional Italian pizza that comes from Naples. It’s known for its simple and fresh ingredients, representing the colors of the Italian flag.The pizza has a thin, soft crust that’s slightly crispy on the edges. It’s usually baked in a wood-fired oven, which gives it a unique flavor. A drizzle of olive oil is often added before or after baking.It’s one of the most famous and classic pizzas in the world.

main ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce (made from fresh or canned tomatoes)
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Love

How to make it

  1. Make the dough (or use pre-made).Mix flour, water, yeast, salt, and a little olive oil.Knead until smooth and elastic and let it rise for 1-2 hours until double the size.
  2. Preheat the oven:Set it to a high temperature (around 220–250°C or 425–480°F)
  3. Shape the dough:Roll or stretch the dough into a round shape on a floured surface.Place it on a baking tray or pizza stone.
  4. Add the toppings:Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce on the dough.Add fresh mozzarella slices on top.Drizzle with a little olive oil.
  5. Bake the pizza:Place the pizza in the oven and bake for 8–12 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
  6. Finish and serve:Add fresh basil leaves on top after baking.
  7. Slice and enjoy your homemade pizza!
